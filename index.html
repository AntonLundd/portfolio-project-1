Welcome to Stockholm Book Club!

We are a group of book lovers who meet the first saturday each month to discuss and share our thoughts on the book we are currently reading.
Our meetings are held at Stockholms stadsbibliotek and are open to all interested readers. The current book we are reading is:

The Da Vinci Code by Dan Brown

Past book discussions:

  • To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee
  • The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald
  • Don Quijote by Miguel Cervantes

Sign up to our weekly newsletter

We send out an email with tips on new and upcoming books, aswell as information around our upcoming meeting.
